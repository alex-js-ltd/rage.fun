### How does magicmint.fun work?

magicmint.fun generates bonding curves to raise liquidity for token launches on Raydium.

Each curve leverages a Rust implementation of the Bancor Formula—enabling built-in price discovery and a liquidity mechanism for newly created tokens. This system creates a positive feedback loop, where the price of tokens increases as the supply grows.

When tokens are purchased, the payment is added to the reserve balance and, based on the calculated price, **new tokens are minted** to the buyer.

Similarly, when tokens are sold, they are **removed from the supply** (burned), and based on the current price, reserve tokens (lamports) are transferred to the seller.

The supply of each token **expands or contracts automatically based on market demand.**

Every token on magicmint.fun has a max supply of 1 billion.

### Does magicmint.fun have trading fees?

Every buy and sell transaction includes a 1% trading fee.

### What happens when the bonding curve is complete?

Once users have minted a total of 888,888,888 tokens, the bonding curve is complete. At this point:

- An additional 111,111,112 tokens are automatically minted to the Raydium vault, bringing the total supply to 1 billion.
- Mint authority is revoked.
- A Constant Product Pool is automatically deployed on Raydium with a ratio of 111,111,112 tokens and the SOL reserve amount chosen by the creator.
- Token swaps will be available on Jupiter and Raydium.
